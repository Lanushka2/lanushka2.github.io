Let me introduce myself: my name is Lana, I am a psychologist, gestalt therapist, working with clients since 2012.

I graduated from the Faculty of Psychology, Lomonosov Moscow State University, passed more than 1000 hours of additional education (confirmed by certificates).

I INVITE TO THERAPY those who want to improve relationships with loved ones or colleagues, resolve conflicts, increase self-esteem and self-confidence, learn to defend themselves, understand their desires and needs, go through a difficult situation, get support and professional help.

The duration of therapy depends on the topic with which the person comes. Some situational issues (for example, a conflict with a boss) can be resolved in 1–5 meetings, others (for example, a change in some character traits) may require long-term work.

I work online individually as well as with couples and families.
You can contact me through WhatsApp +7 (915) 479-6840.

MORE ABOUT THE TOPICS I work with:

  • relationships with parents, children, other family members
  • relationship in a couple
  • relationships with colleagues, friends, bosses and other relationships on equal or unequal positions
  • self-esteem, insecurity, rejection of oneself, difficulties in self-presentation, defending oneself and one's interests, the ability to say "no"
  • difficulties in understanding your own desires and needs, difficulty in making decisions
  • laziness, decreased motivation
  • emotional addiction
  • traumatic situations (loss of a loved one, emotional or physical abuse), difficult life situations, fear of an upcoming important event
  • depression, high anxiety, depression, panic attacks, unbearable feelings (aggression, fear, phobias)
  • difficulties in the sexual sphere
